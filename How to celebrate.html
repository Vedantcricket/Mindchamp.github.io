How To Celebrate Oktoberfest ?

These are some different ways you can celebrate Oktoberfest at home

  • Create your own Oktoberfest decorations to get into the spirit of things.
  • Prepare Oktoberfest themed food or get Oktoberfest catering.
  • drinking lots of german beers
  • Have a "Prost!" (Cheers!) with your friends.
  • dancing
  • Games
  • Decorate your space in traditional Oktoberfest style.
  • wearing traditional clothes
  • Encourage male guests to wear traditional clothes like lederhosen.
  • Let female guests come in their best dirndls.
